This humanoid is about the size of a gnome or halfling. It has a scaly hide,
a naked tail like that of a rat, and a doglike head with two small horns.
Kobolds are short, reptilian humanoids with cowardly and sadistic
tendencies.
A kobold’s scaly skin ranges from dark rusty brown to a rusty
black color. It has glowing red eyes. Its tail is nonprehensile.
Kobolds wear ragged clothing, favoring red and orange.
Kobolds usually consume plants or animals but are not averse to
eating intelligent beings. They spend most of their time fortifying
the land around their lairs with traps and warning devices (such as
spiked pits, tripwires attached to crossbows, and other mechanical
contraptions).
