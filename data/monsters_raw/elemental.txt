ELEMENTAL
A ghaele can take the form of an incorporeal globe of eldritch
colors, 5 feet in diameter. Elementals are incarnations of the elements that compose existence.
A ghaele is about 6 feet tall and weighs about 170 pounds. They are as wild and dangerous as the forces that birthed them.
Ghaeles speak Celestial, Infernal, and Draconic, but can com-
municate with almost any creature, thanks to their tongues ability. COMBAT
